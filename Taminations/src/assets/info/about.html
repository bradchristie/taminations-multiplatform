Welcome to Taminations

Here you will find animations for square dance calls from Mainstream through C-3B. Most calls have several animations from different starting positions. Taminations are written and maintained by Brad Christie, webmaster of the Tam Twirlers Square Dance Club.

Taminations is not affiliated with CALLERLAB. For official and complete definitions, refer to the CALLERLAB documents. For Challenge definitions, the web site and books by Vic Ceder are another important reference.

Call pronunciations provided by Eric Henerlau.

What's New

  • More animations for Wheel the Ocean, Jaywalk, Square Chain Thru, Split Counter Rotate, Concentric Pass In/Out, O Split Counter Rotate, O Rotary Spin, Tandem Right and Left Thru, Step and Fold, Dixie Grand, Explode the Line.
  • Sequencer improvements:
    • Fix for some calls directed to centers of a tidal wave.
    • Improvements for Brace Thru.
    • Add Slide In/Out/Left/Right for manually moving dancers.
    • Enable Triangle Circulate from sausage.
    • Grand Swing Thru for waves of 6.
