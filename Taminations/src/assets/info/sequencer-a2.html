A-2 Calls Coded for the Sequencer

These are the A-2 calls that are programmed for the sequencer, and should work from any appropriate formation.
  • Box Counter Rotate
  • Single Wheel
  • Slip, Slide, Swing, Slither
  • Spin the Windmill In/Out/Left/Right. You can also use formations where the centers are facing couples, and with Left Spin the Windmill they will step to a left-hand wave.
  • Transfer and <anything>
  • Zig Zag family
