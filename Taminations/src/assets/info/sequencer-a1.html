A-1 Calls Coded for the Sequencer

These are the A-1 calls that are programmed for the sequencer, and should work from any appropriate formation.
  • As Couples - You cannot limit As Couples to just some dancers: "Boys As Couples Run" will not work.
  • Beaus and Belles
  • Brace Thru
  • Clover and Anything
  • Cross
  • Cross Over Circulate - in addition to any general line, can also be applied to 4 selected dancers.
  • Double Star Thru and Triple Star Thru
  • Partner Tag
  • Pass In and Pass Out
  • Quarter In and Quarter Out
  • Right/Left Roll to a Wave
  • Split Square Thru
  • Triple Trade
