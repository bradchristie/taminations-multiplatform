Reverse

The modifier "Reverse" applies to certain calls and interchanges Beau and Belle and the directions of clockwise and counterclockwise (and also right and left). At Advanced, dancers are expected to apply Reverse to the calls listed below, and other applications of Reverse should be workshopped or avoided.

  1. Rollaway
  2. Half Sashay
  3. Wheel Around
  4. Courtesy Turn
  5. Flutterwheel
  6. Dixie Style to an Ocean Wave
  7. Circle to a Line
  8. Single Circle to a Wave
  9. Swap Around
  10. Recycle (from Facing Couples)
